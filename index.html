// Who I Am

The Mind Behind NitroFX

Gautam Deshmukh

Video Editor · Photographer · Creative Director

NitroFX is more than a name — it's a creative force in the Indian cinematography scene. With over three years of professional experience, I've had the privilege of working with top-tier cinematography organizations and some of India's biggest content creators.

From cinematic documentaries like "LET'S BE UNDERDOG AGAIN" for Genesis Esports, to editing vlogs for Dynamo Gaming across Singapore, I bring a cinematic eye to every project. My work spans video editing, creative ideation, graphic design, and professional event photography.

I believe every frame tells a story. Every edit matters. Every video is a visual experience that can make or break a brand. That's the standard I hold myself to.
