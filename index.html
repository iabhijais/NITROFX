// Who I Am

The Mind Behind NitroFX

Gautam Deshmukh

Video Editor · Photographer · Creative Director

NitroFX is more than a name — it's a creative force in the Indian esports scene. With over two years of professional experience, I've had the privilege of working with top-tier esports organizations and some of India's biggest content creators.

From cinematic documentaries like "LET'S BE UNDERDOG AGAIN" for Genesis Esports, to editing vlogs for Dynamo Gaming across Singapore, I bring a cinematic eye to every project. My work spans video editing, thumbnail design, creative ideation, and professional esports event photography.

I believe every frame tells a story. Every edit matters. Every thumbnail is a first impression that can make or break a video. That's the standard I hold myself to.

// What I Do

Services & Expertise

End-to-end creative solutions for esports organizations, gaming content creators, and brands in the gaming space.

🎬

Video Editing

Cinematic esports documentaries, tournament recaps, podcast series, vlogs and brand content. High-energy edits with professional color grading and motion graphics.

🖼️

Thumbnail Design

Eye-catching, click-worthy thumbnails for YouTube and social media. Bold typography, dynamic compositions, and designs that stop the scroll and boost CTR.

📸

Esports Photography

Professional event photography capturing the intensity and emotion of competitive gaming. LAN events, team photos, behind-the-scenes, and player portraits.

💡

Creative Ideation

🎨

Graphic Design

Social media graphics, match result cards, player spotlights, announcement posts, and branded content for esports organizations and content creators.

🎯

Content Strategy

Strategic planning for content creators and esports orgs. YouTube optimization, posting schedules, visual identity development, and audience growth tactics.
